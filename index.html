About E-Aid
According to the Bangladesh Bureau of Statistics (BBS) [4 January, 2020] about 4,71,000 people died in the last 5 years in Bangladesh due to the lack of medical treatment. The main object of our project is to take the treatment system of Bangladesh a step forward. Predominantly, we have worked to solve various obstacles to the treatment system of Bangladesh.

Content Details: Our project is mainly an app-based project associated with our main website. We have planned the project to provide the following services:

Main Services: One patient can give an online serial for appointment of doctors from any place of Bangladesh by using our app. Here the hassle of the patients will be reduced as there will a vivid clarification about the confirmation, itâ€™s cost-free. And a patient can easily check whether the doctor is available or not. A patient can call for a pharmacist to come home and provide medical treatment anytime. It is mainly for first aid service and for those who need a little medical treatment. This service will be provided by the pharmacists within a short time. A user can order medicine by using our app. These medicines will be delivered by our service providers within 30 minutes to 1 hour. The quality of the medicines will be ensured as much as possible.

Emergency Service: This service is for the critical patients. In this case, they can find the number of nearby hospitals and 24/7 service providing ambulance contact numbers. Besides, they can book ambulances by using our app.

Special Services:
(01) Volunteering Team Service: We will choose 20-30 persons from different families. They will be given first aid training through online mainly. But there will be a monthly indoctrination where they will be taught practically.
(02) Charitable Fund: For people an online fund will be raised. In this case, proper proof will be needed. The money of that fund will be given to them for their medical treatment. A regular fund will be added from our profit also.
(03) Blood Bank: There will remain an online blood bank service in our app. In this section, the doners and receivers can contact to each other directly.
